About

Born and raised in Japan, Italy and Germany! I have love and respect for Animation for as long as I can remember. I started YouTubing as a kid and gained a big following which motivated me to further study my craft. I have completed my studies in Animation at the Filmakademie Animationinstitut in Ludwigsburg, Germany in early 2024 and Animation Mentor in 2025.

I acquired knowledge in 2D Animation, Editing, Directing, Lead Animation and CG Animation. I had the amazing opportunity to have private mentors from studios like Pixar and Dreamworks to work on my 3D Animation craft. Observing interesting things in life and putting these into my animations and hopefully entertaining each and everyone is my goal!
